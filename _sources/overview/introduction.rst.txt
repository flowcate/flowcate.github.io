Overview
########

The DeepHub® is the premier reference implementation of an omlox™ hub.
It allows for seamless tracking across different positioning technologies, and facilitates all location-aware use cases in smart production and smart logistics.
It is compatible with the `omlox™ standard <https://omlox.com/>`_ - the open location standard for Industry 4.0.

To get access to a shared cloud instance on AWS or to test drive a local on-premise instance of the DeepHub, contact our Partner Management team at partners@flowcate.com.

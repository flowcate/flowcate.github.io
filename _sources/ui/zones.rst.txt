
Zones
+++++

.. card:: Step 1

    To access the Zones menu, click the gear icon at the top right corner of the DeepHub® UI to open the Configuration menu. Click on "Zones".
    
    .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_1.png
        :class: screenshot


.. card:: Step 2

    To create a new localization zone, click on “New Zone” and a new pop-up window will be launched to specify characteristics of the zone.

    .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_2.png
        :class: screenshot

    Fill in the name of the zone and the floor level upon which the zone is to be created.

    Select the desired technology from the dropdown menu.


.. card:: Step 3

    Select a method of creating a zone from the dropdown list and click on "Next". There are 4 methods to create a zone.

    .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_3.png
        :class: screenshot


.. card:: Step 3.1

    .. tab-set::
        .. tab-item:: Extent based geometries

            **Extent based geometries (height/width)**
            

            Enter the height and width of the zone rectangle in meters. It is also possible to enter the approximate address where the zone is to be created.
            Click on "Next" and the zone will appear on the user interface or at the approximate location that was specified.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_4.png
                :class: screenshot

            Adjust the zone orientation by moving and rotating the zone and click on "Save" to update the zone on the user interface.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_5.png
                :class: screenshot

        .. tab-item:: Local Position Input

            **Local Position Input (Four-sided Polygon)**

            Enter the coordinates for each corner of a rectangular zone in meters. It is also possible to enter the approximate address where the zone is to be created.
            Click on "Next" and the zone will appear on the user interface or at the approximate location that was specified.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_6.png
                :class: screenshot

            Adjust the zone orientation by moving and rotating the zone and click on "Save" to update the zone on the user interface.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_5.png
                :class: screenshot

        .. tab-item:: GPS Position Input

            **GPS Position Input (Four-sided Polygon)**
            
            Enter the geographic coordinates for each corner of a rectangular zone in the form of longitude and latitude.
            Click on "Next" and the zone will appear on the user interface.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_7.png
                :class: screenshot

            Adjust the zone orientation by moving and rotating the zone and click on "Save" to update the zone on the user interface.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_5.png
                :class: screenshot

        .. tab-item:: Drawing on map

            **Drawing on map**

            Locate the desired site on the map using the search bar. Left click on the map to draw a polygonal zone in any shape. For example: Click 4 points to draw a rectangular zone.
            On the right side of the user interface, the local position for each point of the newly introduced zone can be modified.
            Click on the “Save” button to integrate the new zone into the map.

            .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_8.png
                :class: screenshot

.. card:: Step 4

    The newly created zone will appear on the map. On the left side pane, the list of created zones will appear.

    .. image:: images/hub_i_zone_9.png
        :class: screenshot
